Tips for Proper Laptop Usage

  • Maintain good posture while using your laptop.
  • Take regular breaks to avoid eye strain.
  • Keep your laptop on a flat, hard surface to prevent overheating.
  • Regularly clean your keyboard and screen to ensure hygiene and performance.
  • Use a reliable antivirus to protect your device from malware.
